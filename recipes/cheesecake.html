New York Cheesecake

Why cook this dish?

This authentic, creamy desert will add a taste of New York to any dining room table.

This American cheesecake is a perfect way to end a meal

Ingredients:

  • 85g Butter
  • 140g Digestive biscuits
  • 1tbsp Sugar
  • 900g Philadelphia Cheese
  • 250g Caster Sugar
  • 3tbsp Plain flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 tsp Lemon Juice
  • 3 Large eggs
  • 284ml Sour cream
